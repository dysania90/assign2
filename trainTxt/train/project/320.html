three key components developments in the core of user interface toolkits eg constraint programming developments developments in multimedia systems and toolkits eg the command stream and synchme multimedia toolkit applications and joint research projects the joint research projects include grouplens collaborative filtering for usenet news with john riedl and the brain neighborhood viewer for
