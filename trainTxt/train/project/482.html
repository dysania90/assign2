generalization of period is defined for cyclic motions that makes periodic variation explicit this representation called the period trace is compact and purely temporal describing the evolution of an object or scene without reference to spatial quantities such as position or velocity by delimiting cycles and identifying correspondences across cycles the period trace provides a means of temporally
