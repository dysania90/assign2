innovation in computer software building autonomous agents that interact with realworld software environments such as operating systems or databases is a pragmatically convenient yet intellectually challenging substrate for ai research to support this claim we are utilizing planning and machinelearning techniques to develop an internet softbot software robot a customizable and moderately intelligent assistant for internet access the softbot accepts goals in a highlevel language generates and executes plans to achieve these goals and learns from its experience the softbot enables a human user to state what he or she wants accomplished the softbot disambiguates the request and dynamically determines how and where to satisfy it the softbot uses a unix shell and the worldwide web
