finished his phd from the graphics visualization and usability center of the college of computing at the georgia institute of technology in 1995 his research advisor was prof jim foley at present he is working in the campc research lab of nec research his research interests include scientific software and information visualization his dissertation was concerned with developing strategies for efficient visualization of the information space of hypermedia systems he developed a tool called the navigational view builder for letting the designer develop effective views of the worldwideweb before this he was involved in developing a visual debugger called lens he was also part of the team that developed glyphmaker publications in georgia tech mukherjea and stasko applying algorithm
