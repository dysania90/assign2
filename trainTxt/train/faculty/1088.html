hardware with little performance and area penalty in another project we are investigating ways to use builtin selftest hardware to test a system while it is performing its normal operation the goal is that the system is tested continuously as it operates with little or no impact on system performance much of our work is performed using facilities of the vlsi digital system laboratory the laboratory
