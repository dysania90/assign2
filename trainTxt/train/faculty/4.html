in 1980 the ms degree in electrical and computer engineering and the phd degree in computer and information science from the university of massachusetts amherst mass in 1984 and 1987 respectively his published extensively in these areas and is author of the book coordination of distributed problem solvers kluwer academic press in his most recent work he has been designing a framework for coordination based on hierarchical multidimensional behavior specifications and he has been developing an integrating architecture for combining realtime and intelligent systems he is a 1991 recipient of a presidential young investigator award from the national science foundation prior to joining the faculty at the university of michigan in 1988 he was a research computer scientist in the
