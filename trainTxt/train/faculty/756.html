melanie mitchell professor melanie mitchell received a phd from the university of michigan in 1990 in addition to her academic position at the university of new mexico she is a research professor and director of the adaptive computation program at the santa fe institute santa fe new mexico her research interests include intelligent systems machine learning and parallel and distributed computation
