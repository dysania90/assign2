I was born on October 11, 1967, in Athens, Greece. In 1990, I graduated from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the University of Patras, Greece. In 1991, I joined the Ph.D. program of UCSD receiving my MS one year later. My area of interest is theoretical computer science and my Ph.D advisor is Russell Impagliazzo. I believe in those who study their science because of love for her, against the usual - pressures for hasty results - vain antagonisms for convenient recognition - unethical concessions for carreer improvement and insist on - respecting the scientific research - trying to unserstand clearly even what is already known - striving for learning - being free.
