Introduction I am a Ph.D. candidate at UT-Austin in the Department of Computer Sciences. My supervising professor is Jayadev Misra and my dissertation topic is Real-Time UNITY. I am a member of Professor Misra's PSP research group. I am extending the UNITY theory in order to express finite time bounds on the usual UNITY operators for progress and safety. I am also interested in functional programming languages, partial order semantics and automated theorem proving.
