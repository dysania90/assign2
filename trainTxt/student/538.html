A PostScript version of my (outdated) resume is here. Research I am interested in solving real-world classification and prediction problems using machine learning and other data analysis techniques. My current domain of interest is medical image analysis . I am very interested in knowledge discovery from data repositories. Ph.D. Thesis I spent a lot of time putting up my Ph.D. thesis online. As a guest at this site, it is your moral obligation to check it out! There is a HTML version that allows selective retrieval of just about any portion you want.
