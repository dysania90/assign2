The NDR group at the University of Michigan is actively conducting research in diverse areas of VLSI design, testing and CAD. We are part of the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science department of the College of Engineering. The group is headed by Professor Pinaki Mazumder. Current research thrusts are in the areas of Quantum Circuit Design, CAD, Memory Testing, Built-In Self-Repair, and Flat Panel Display Electronics. Quantum circuit development is a collaborative effort with the Center for High Frequency Microelectronics at the University of Michigan. The project is under the direction of Professor George I. Haddad, Chair, EECS department and is jointly funded by ARPA and URI. Research Assistantships Available Three (3) research assistantships are available.
