Areas include logic synthesis, technology mapping, physical design, testing, and yield enhancement for various VLSI technologies such as standard cells, gate arrays, field programmable gate arrays (FPGAs) and multi-chip modules (MCMs). Other areas of study include a structural theory of the large-scale global optimizations which arise in VLSI CAD. People of the VLSI CAD Lab Faculty: Jason Cong, Andrew B.
