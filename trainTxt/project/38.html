The group works with Dr. Larry Dowdy in the field of Performance Evaluation of Computer Systems. Current research interests of the group include performance prediction models, workload characterization, multiprocessor scheduling, modeling of parallel I/O (RAID disk systems) and experimental studies of parallel systems.
